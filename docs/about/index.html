About

This project was initiated by Asep Bagja Priandana and Retno Ika Safitri. We are founders of Tanibox, an agriculture technology company in Indonesia.

The main reason why Tania exist is to scratch our own itch when managing our small farm in our house. If we can solve our problem, so why not try to offer our solution to another farmer? We want to contribute more to the farmer communities around the world.

Tania is maintained by Tanibox and licensed under Apache 2.0 open source.
